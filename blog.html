Gritly's Origin Story

Our CEO, David, lived through this problem. As a first-gen, low-income student, he was lucky to make it to a big university. That’s where he was exposed to just how wide the gap was between underestimated students back home, training, and entry-level career opportunities. He set out to solve this issue. The founding team is composed of David Lopez and Avi Uddaraju, who is CTO. David and Avi have been working together for over two years.
‍
David, the CEO, is all about leadership, sales, and ensuring our team has the resources to succeed. Avi is our technical lead and has the experience necessary to build the infrastructure to set our stakeholders up for success. Both founders have had multiple experiences with startups + accelerators before teaming up to build Gritly. We have the right team to solve this problem. Along with a persistence and execution mindset, we are technically capable of serving our students with the best student experience and tools needed. Along with building partnerships with the companies and organizations, we will push our mission forward regardless because we understand that everything we are working on is bigger than ourselves. This is all possible because of how closely we understand our students, as we were them, dealing with hardships life throws at us and lack of opportunities to shine.

What is Gritly?

We are a bunch of curious and overly charming sales experts, who are here to help you become one among us! We provide basics - advanced sales curriculum, designed to train and provide our students with a direct sneak into how a career in the sales sector  in the US looks like.
‍
Gritly is a career kickstarter bootcamp program, focused on enabling diverse communities into entry-level roles, starting with tech sales.
‍
 - Minorities want to break into sales but can’t afford traditional business degrees; companies want to be more diverse but can’t find qualified minority talent.
‍
Our goal is simple. We empower, educate and train students through sales bootcamps in the US regions to prepare them for the most in-demand job in the country - Sales. And not only that, we also help them network with our partner companies and ensure they land up in their most dreamed sales jobs.
